> Uzunjati understand that **knowledge** lives in stories passed down through the generations and also is earned every day through personal experience.
> Members of the Uzunjati, the oldest branch the Magaambya, are maintainers of peace and the guardians of thousands of storytelling traditions. Their name is an ancient Mwangi word for a story or collection of knowledge. Uzunjati weave captivating tales, drawing out lessons they apply to their diplomacy.
