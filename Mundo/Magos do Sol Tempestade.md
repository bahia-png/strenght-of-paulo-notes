> Tempest-Sun Mages embody **valor**, not only the physical courage to stand up for friends and the school, but also the intellectual courage to consider opposing viewpoints and widen one’s understanding rather than staying entrenched in a single paradigm.
> Tempest-Sun Mages defend the Magaambya’s holdings, members, and allies. More wardens than warriors, Tempest-Sun Mages end conflict using spell and speech.
